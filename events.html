Events & Programs

Project CHAnDAnA organizes various events and programs to raise awareness about Sickle Cell Anemia, provide screening services, and engage with communities. Check out our upcoming events and past activities.

World Sickle Cell Awareness Day

10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
District Hospitals in Mysuru, Kodagu, and Chamarajanagar

Join us for a day of awareness, education, and free screening for Sickle Cell Anemia. The event will feature expert talks, interactive sessions, and screening camps.

Mobile Screening Camp

June 15-20, 2025
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Tribal Hamlets, Mysuru District

Our mobile screening units will visit tribal hamlets in Mysuru district to provide free screening for Sickle Cell Anemia. The camp will also include awareness sessions and distribution of educational materials.
