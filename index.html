Project CHAnDAnA

Project CHAnDAnA is a comprehensive healthcare mission to eliminate Sickle Cell Anemia. It is a combined effort of IOCL (Indian Oil), Govt of Karnataka, and the Indian Institute of Science (Govt institute).

The project aims to screen approximately 2.56 lakh tribal individuals over two years to identify and manage cases of sickle cell anaemia in Mysuru, Kodagu, and Chamarajanagar districts.

Read More

What is Sickle Cell Anemia?

A genetic disorder affecting hemoglobin in red blood cells, leading to various health complications such as anaemia, acute and chronic pain, infections, and more.
